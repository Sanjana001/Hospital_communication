Online Hospital

Online Hospital provides you a platform for a hospital to communicate with the hospital staff and doctors. This platform provides you the following aids:

  • Confirming or Appointing the appointment with the doctor
  • Getting the prescriptions online
  • Getting all the information about the problem, disease, doctor, reports or medicines
  • Effective Communication between hospital staff and patient

Frequently Asked Questions

what is Online patient portal?

A patient portal is a secure online website that gives you convenient 24-hour access to your personal health information. Patients can electronically access all their health treatments and their details using monitored dashboards provided by the hospital.

What can I do with a patient portal?

  • Exchange secure emails with your health care team
  • Schedule non-urgent/urgent appointments
  • Get Information regarding your's appointed doctor

How I can schedule an appointment with the doctor

Click on the CREATE AN ACCOUNT button provided on the hospital's website.Provide your informaition like Name and Email-id and set a password to create an account.
After that,a dashboard will be procvided to you through which you can create an appointment.

What are some of the benefits of a patient portal?

Patients benefit from more efficient and effective communication with providers as well as better and timelier self-care.

How do I get access to a patient portal?

Your health care providers can provide you with instructions to set up your account. There may be a couple of steps involved, including creating a secure password to make sure that only you have access to your health information.
